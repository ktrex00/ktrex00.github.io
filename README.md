## About Me

I have always followed my passions where they have led me. I began my professional life working in animal care, from small, rehabilitation facilities to large companies like Disney. I have spent over 10 years in this field, relishing the challenges that come with it. Animal care is unique among many jobs due to the versatility that is necessary to provide a high level of care. Many problems require creative and individual solutions, an aspect which has always engaged me.

Upon learning about the field of Data Science, I realized that my knack for statistics and coding could be used to explore deeper into the historical data that already existed. I have been using these skills with animal data that has been recorded over the years to find patterns and insights that are not immediately obvious to human eyes. This portfolio contains information about some of the projects I have worked on.
